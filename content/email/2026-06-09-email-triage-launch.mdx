Rocky reads your Gmail and surfaces what actually needs attention — no inbox-opening required.

## What it does

Send Rocky a WhatsApp message like *"What emails need my attention today?"* and get back a ranked digest: urgent replies, follow-ups, and FYIs — nothing more.

## Key capabilities

- **Priority triage** — scores each email by sender, urgency signals, and thread age
- **Natural language search** — "find the email from Shyam about the NDA" works across all connected accounts
- **Multi-account** — handles multiple Gmail accounts in one conversation
- **Draft & send** — dictate a reply in WhatsApp, Rocky drafts and sends it

## How to use it

> "Summarise my unread emails from the last 24 hours"

> "Find the thread with Harshith about the pitch deck"

> "Draft a reply to Sudeep saying I'll confirm by Thursday"
