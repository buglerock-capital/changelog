Share any image with Rocky in WhatsApp — a document, chart, business card, or screenshot — and get a structured analysis instantly.

## What it does

Rocky uses vision AI to read and interpret images. Share a photo of a whiteboard meeting, a scanned document, or a screenshot of a conversation and Rocky will extract the content, summarise it, and let you act on it.

## Key capabilities

- **Document reading** — PDFs, scanned pages, photos of printed text
- **Chart & data interpretation** — financial charts, tables, infographics
- **Business card extraction** — name, email, phone, company — saved to contacts
- **Screenshot analysis** — share an app screenshot and ask Rocky what it means

## How to use it

Share an image, then ask:

> "What does this chart say about Q2 performance?"

> "Extract all the contact details from this business card"

> "Summarise the key points in this document photo"
