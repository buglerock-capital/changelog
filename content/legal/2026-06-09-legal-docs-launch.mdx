Rocky generates org-approved legal documents from WhatsApp — no lawyer handoff required for standard templates.

## What it does

Request a document by type and provide the key details. Rocky fills in the approved template, generates a DOCX (or PDF), and sends a secure download link directly in WhatsApp.

## Key capabilities

- **Template-bound** — Rocky fills fields only; it cannot author arbitrary legal text, ensuring every document stays within approved language
- **DOCX + PDF output** — editable Word file by default; PDF available on request
- **Secure delivery** — HMAC-signed download links, accessible only to the requesting user
- **Audit trail** — every generated document is logged with timestamp, requester, and parameters

## Available templates

- Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
- Mutual NDA
- Engagement Letter (in progress)

## How to use it

> "Generate an NDA for Prestige Group, mutual, governed by Singapore law"

> "Create an NDA for a new hire starting Monday, confidentiality period 2 years"
