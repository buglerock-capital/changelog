Rocky connects the tools your team already uses — Asana, Google Workspace, and your monitoring stack — and makes them accessible from WhatsApp.

## What it does

Create Asana tasks, run Google Workspace automations, and get structured audit logs of every AI action — without leaving your phone.

## Asana integration

Rocky has direct access to your Asana workspace (BugleRock Holdings, GID 5298461875554):

- **Create tasks** — assign to team members, set due dates, attach to projects
- **List & search** — "What tasks are due this week?" or "Show Harshith's open tasks"
- **Update & complete** — mark tasks done, change due dates, add comments
- **Project view** — list all active projects with status

> "Add a task for Harshith to review the pitch deck by Friday, in the IB pipeline project"

## Google Workspace automation

Rocky can execute pre-approved GWS scripts against BigQuery, Sheets, Drive, and other services via a sandboxed shell — no manual script runs needed.

## Audit & compliance

- Every LLM call is logged with input/output for compliance review
- Admin-gated tools require explicit approval before execution
- OAuth tokens encrypted at rest; HMAC-signed artifact URLs
- Full access log available at `/admin/usage` on the dashboard
