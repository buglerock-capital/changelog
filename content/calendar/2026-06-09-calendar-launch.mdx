Rocky connects to Google Calendar so you can manage your schedule entirely over WhatsApp — no app switching needed.

## What it does

Ask Rocky what your day looks like, find a free slot, or create an event with attendees. It handles timezone offsets, recurring meetings, and conflict detection automatically.

## Key capabilities

- **Schedule view** — "What does my Thursday look like?" returns a clean summary
- **Availability search** — "Find 30 min for Manoj and me this week" checks both calendars
- **Event creation** — creates events with attendees, reminders, and descriptions from a single message

## How to use it

> "What meetings do I have tomorrow?"

> "Block 2 hours Thursday afternoon for deep work"

> "Schedule a call with Sudhindra and Vikas on Friday at 4pm IST"
