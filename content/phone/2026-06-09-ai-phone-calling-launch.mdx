Rocky can make phone calls on your behalf — briefed with context, speaking naturally, and reporting back with a full summary.

## What it does

Tell Rocky to call someone and what to discuss. It dials using Bland AI's voice engine, carries the conversation with the context you provided, and sends you a transcript and key takeaways when the call ends.

## Key capabilities

- **Context injection** — Rocky is briefed on who you're calling and why before it dials
- **Timezone-aware scheduling** — knows when it's appropriate to call based on the callee's timezone
- **Post-call summary** — transcript + bullet-point outcomes delivered to WhatsApp
- **Call history** — all calls logged with outcome notes for reference

## How to use it

> "Call Rohan and confirm the meeting is still on for Friday"

> "Call the office and let them know I'll be 20 minutes late"

> "Call Vickram and get an update on the term sheet"
