Rocky is BugleRock's internal AI assistant, built to run entirely over WhatsApp. No dashboards, no logins, no context-switching — just message Rocky and it gets done.

Everything is accessible from a single conversation: emails, calendar, phone calls, documents, images, and more.

## What's shipped

- [Smart Inbox Triage & Email Search](/email/2026-06-09-email-triage-launch) — read, search, draft and send emails from WhatsApp
- [Calendar Management](/calendar/2026-06-09-calendar-launch) — schedule events, check availability and manage your day
- [AI Phone Calling](/phone/2026-06-09-ai-phone-calling-launch) — make outbound calls with full context, get back a summary
- [Voice Notes](/voice/2026-06-09-voice-notes-launch) — send a voice message, get a transcript and action items
- [Image Intelligence](/images/2026-06-09-image-intelligence-launch) — share any image and get a structured analysis
- [Legal Doc Generation](/legal/2026-06-09-legal-docs-launch) — generate NDAs and agreements in seconds
- [Enterprise Infrastructure](/enterprise/2026-06-09-enterprise-infra-launch) — Asana, Google Workspace and audit logging
